FAQ

Crypto Signal FAQ — Questions? We have answers.

What is Valo AI and how does it work?+
Valo AI is a WhatsApp-first AI trading assistant for cryptocurrency. Our system monitors 200+ coin pairs around the clock using multi-indicator analysis — Bollinger Bands, RSI, MACD, Moving Averages, and Volume. When the AI identifies a high-quality setup with strong confluence, it automatically broadcasts a complete, structured signal directly to your WhatsApp. No action required from you.
Do I need to do anything to receive signals?+
No. After connecting your WhatsApp number in your dashboard, signals are delivered to you automatically. Valo AI runs 24/7 — you don't need to monitor charts, log in, or trigger anything. The signal arrives when the market creates a clean setup. You simply open WhatsApp, review it, and decide whether to execute.
Is Valo AI suitable for beginners?+
Yes. The structured format removes all complexity. You don't need to understand technical analysis. Every signal arrives pre-analyzed with a clear entry zone, defined stop loss, three take profit targets, leverage recommendation, position size guidance, and expected duration. All you do is decide whether to follow it on your exchange.
Is this financial advice?+
No. Valo AI provides structured AI-generated market analysis for informational and educational purposes only. Nothing published or delivered by Valo AI constitutes financial advice or a recommendation to trade. Cryptocurrency trading involves substantial risk. You are fully responsible for your own trading decisions.
Which coins and exchanges are supported?+
Valo AI V1 covers 200+ USDT pairs using Binance market data. Popular assets including BTC, ETH, SOL, XRP, BNB, and a broad altcoin library are monitored continuously. Bybit and MEXC data is on the roadmap for V2. The pair library grows as new assets qualify under our signal quality threshold.
How often do signals arrive?+
Signal frequency depends on market conditions. Valo AI only broadcasts when a high-quality setup is identified — not on a fixed schedule. In active markets, you may receive multiple signals per day across different pairs. Quality over quantity is the principle: every signal that arrives has passed our confluence threshold.
What makes Valo different from Telegram signal groups?+
Most signal groups give vague entries with no stop loss or position sizing. Valo AI delivers fully structured setups — calculated entry zones, defined stop loss placement, three take profit levels, leverage sized to the risk distance, position size guidance, and a hard risk rule (max 2 trades, 20% total exposure). Structure and risk management are the entire product.
